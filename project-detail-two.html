Project Overview

SmartPots is a fictitious smart plant care device that comes with a remote app.

The goal of this project is to construct an app, website, and responsive email for our fictitious product. Students may utilise any software to create an interactive app, but the website and responsive email must be manually built.

01 Ideatation & Information
Gathering

General research on the subject and existing competitors. Use analysis approaches like brainstorming, research, and information collecting to create personas. Define product functions and client segments.


Product information is fictitious and based on the creativity and investigation of team members. Unsplash and Abode Stocks provided royalty-free images. Ensure that all photos adhere to the colour scheme and are appropriate with the product's content.

02 Work Process

Waterfall Methodology

This project uses waterfall approach to assist split the project down into segments. Grantt charts are used by the project manager to track project progress.


Tasks are assigned to team members in an equitable manner, and there is a regular check-in to ensure that the concepts are consistent and that all team members agree on the final deliverables. Every member of the team contributes ideas, facilitates feedback, and actively works on the project.

UI/UX Design

Low Fidelity Wireframes

Before creating the final design, UI/UX designers gathered all of the information from the previous steps, conducted research, and created low-fi wireframes. It took a long time to decide which features and functionalities would be useful to the clients.

High Fidelity Wireframes

The interactivity's high-fi wireframes were altered multiple times to guarantee that consumers had no difficulty clicking certain buttons. Before moving on to the development stage, the entire team thoroughly reviewed all hi-fi wireframes to ensure that all issues had been addressed and that all team members agreed on the final design.

03 Final Product

After addressing the issues discovered during design and testing, the final product was created. The product is completed with the approval of all team members. Problems and issues have been fixed.
