Project Overview

A collaborative project involves assigning tasks and responsibilities to each person and collaborating to develop a fictitious product. The project includes creating product images and brand Identifiers, designing advertisement posters, building an interactive website layout, and defining the company's identity.

The entire project will emphasize design skills, such as using Adobe InDesign to create digital layouts, posters, brand logo.

01 Ideatation & Information
Gathering

Information Gathering

The project's details and content are entirely fictitious. Images are royalty-free and were obtained from Unsplash and Adobe Stocks. During the implementation process, ensure that the visuals and information in the project are appropriate and compatible with the project's content.


The most difficult stage is designing the logo & style tile. The design must ensure that it matches with target audiences.

Mindmap

During this process, we generate concepts and refine product specifications. Because everyone has various opinions, we must pool information from all team members. Using mindmaps and brainstorming allows us to collaborate and offer ideas to create a unique and interesting product.

02 Graphic Design

Style Tile

To minimize dullness, unlike regular flea markets, our flea market is themed around a festival. Because our target audiences are young people and families, we want everything to be colorful and vibrant, from logos to websites to product posters.

Design Posters

One of the primary goals in this project is to create poster layouts that correspond to the product concept as well as the social media platform we use.


Each social media platform will cater to different customer categories. We employed Facebook and Instagram as social media outlets for our project. Our Facebook target audience consists of families, whilst our Instagram target audience consists of youth. Even if the target audiences varies, the design concept must remain consistent with the event subject.

03 Final Product

We utilized the final product's layout to keep the poster's topic consistent. The cherry blossom colour is used throughout the event, from the website to the promotional poster. The image of the poster is altered on each social media site to meet the target clients.
